Who She Is

YUTE LEADER

Angelika Belle is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and performer blending Afro-Reggaeton, R&B, soul, reggae and Afrobeats. Her voice is smooth but grounded, the kind fans describe as natural autotune.

A Theatre Arts graduate of the University of Port Harcourt, she spent six years as lead singer of The Blue Pearls Band before moving to Lagos in 2018 for NYSC. That's when she began posting reggae renditions of Afrobeats songs online. The internet noticed.

The late Sound Sultan discovered her through her reggae cover of "Faya Faya" and signed her to Naija Ninjas, where she released "Sho'Ma" and "Broken or Not." Now independent — and with "Dreadlocks" trending across TikTok — she works on her own terms.
